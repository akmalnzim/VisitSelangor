Hotel Around Selangor


1. Kinrara Resort


Kinrara Resort consists of modern, tropical villas with rooms that lead right up to the outdoor swimming pool. Daily continental buffet breakfast is served at the in-house restaurant.


Location: Bandar Kinrara, Puchong


2. Philea Mines Beach Resort


The vast Philea Mines Beach Resort and its lagoon pool is located next to North Lake, a mining pit lake. Nearby is The Mines Shopping Mall, known for having a canal that passes right through it.


Location: Seri Kembangan


3.The Villas@Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa


Managed by the Sunway Group, you can be assured of world-class hospitality at The Villas@Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa, another resort in Selangor combining modern and tropical design elements.


Location: Sunway
